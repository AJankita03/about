What is Iconista?

Iconista is a powerful, engaging, and beautifully crafted platform that highlights the journey and achievements of women in all walks of life. From pioneers in science, art, politics, sports, literature, to today's changemakers, Iconista brings their stories alive through technology, education, and creativity.
